The Lumber "Jack in a box" quick reference guide.

1 - Craft jack in a box, there's three types; simple, extended and extreme.

2 - Place box on ground

3 - Apply redstone signal

4 - Wait 10 seconds

5 - All wood blocks and leaves are automatically harvested within the range of the block.

Notes:

Jack in a box has been tested with some mods, as long as the ore dictionary lists the item as "wood" and the leaves as "leaves" it should work on them.
